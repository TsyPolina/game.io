About game

This game is based onthe animated television series Gravity Falls. Dipper and his sister MabeL come to Gravity Falls to spend the summer holidays with uncle Stan. Things are not what they seem in this small town, and with the help of a mysterious journal, that Dipper finds in the forest, they begin fighing with supernatural creatures.

Help them defeat all creatures and save the city from destruction.
